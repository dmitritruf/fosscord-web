About Fosscord

Fosscord is free open source software compatible to discord. It is a selfhostable Chat, Voice and Video platform similar to Slack, Rocket.chat and Discord-compatible.

  • Discord-compatible
  • Selfhostable
  • Open Source
  • Configurable
  • Secure
  • Decentralized
  • Extendable
  • Themable

FOSSCORD
